Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Food Truck Finder account and associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account In the App

  • Open the Food Truck Finder app.
  • Go to the Profile tab.
  • Scroll to Account Actions.
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Follow the confirmation steps to permanently delete your account.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account deletion is completed, we delete the account data associated with your profile, which may include:

  • Your account profile information, such as name, email address, and phone number
  • Your saved favorites and alert preferences
  • Your reviews and other user-submitted account content tied to your profile
  • Organizer or owner profile details associated with your account

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Some limited records may be retained temporarily where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or operational backup recovery. If retained, such data is kept only as long as necessary for those purposes.
