# Privacy Policy for FTF: Food Truck Finder

**Effective date:** April 23, 2026  
**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

This is the source/reference copy for the public HTML privacy policy served at:

https://www.ftf-foodtruckfinder.com/privacy.html

Developer and privacy contact: Randy Trimble / Food Truck Finder, foodtruckfinderinfo@gmail.com.

## Summary

Food Truck Finder helps foodies find food trucks and events, helps owners manage truck listings, and helps organizers manage food truck events.

The app may collect or process account information, profile and contact information, location data, truck listing data, event data, uploaded photos/media, user-generated content, favorites and notification preferences, device information, diagnostics, push tokens, and app activity. The exact data depends on whether the user is a foodie, food truck owner, event organizer, or admin/support operator.

The app does not sell personal information.

## Location and Background Location

Food Truck Finder uses location to show nearby trucks and events, calculate distance, support directions, and power live truck discovery.

Food truck owners may enable Live Location. When Live Location is enabled by the owner, Food Truck Finder collects, transmits, syncs, and stores the truck/device location to show customers where that truck is serving, including when the app is not open. Owners can turn Live Location off in the app, and users can manage location permissions in device settings.

## Photos, Notifications, and User Content

Food Truck Finder may request photo library access so users or admins can upload truck photos, menu photos, event images, profile images, and other listing media. Uploaded media may be displayed publicly if used in a public truck or event listing.

The app may request notification permission to send favorite truck alerts, account notifications, owner maintenance reminders, event invites, event responses, and operational updates.

## How Data Is Used

Data is used to:

- Provide nearby food truck and event discovery, maps, search, distance estimates, and directions.
- Create, authenticate, and secure user accounts.
- Support truck owner tools, including profiles, menus, photos, schedules, deals, operating status, and Live Location.
- Support organizer tools, including event creation, event editing, event images, vendor invites, and confirmed vendor tracking.
- Support foodie features, including favorites, alerts, reviews, truck details, event details, and personalization.
- Process uploaded menu images or text when users or admins use menu scanning/transcription features.
- Detect and prevent spam, abuse, fraud, security issues, and policy violations.
- Debug crashes, improve performance, answer support requests, and develop product improvements.

## Sharing and Service Providers

Food Truck Finder does not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app, including cloud hosting, database, storage, authentication, maps, location search, app distribution, push notifications, analytics, crash reporting, support, and AI/menu processing services when used.

Public truck listings, public event listings, public images, public reviews, and other public listing content may be visible to app users.

## Security

Food Truck Finder uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including authenticated accounts, permission controls, role-based administrative access, managed cloud infrastructure, HTTPS/TLS where supported, storage access controls, monitoring, and limited production access for support and operations.

## Retention and Deletion

Account data is generally kept while the account is active. Truck and event listings are retained while active or needed for owner/organizer operations. Live location data is used for live discovery while Live Location is enabled and may be updated, replaced, or marked stale as operations change. Uploaded media is retained while needed for profiles, trucks, menus, events, support, or moderation. Logs, diagnostics, backups, and security records may be retained for reliability, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and backup recovery.

Users can request account deletion:

- In app: Profile tab -> Delete Account.
- Web: https://www.ftf-foodtruckfinder.com/delete-account.html
- Email: foodtruckfinderinfo@gmail.com

When account deletion is completed, personal data associated with the account is deleted or anonymized unless retention is necessary for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, backup recovery, or other legitimate operational purposes.

Depending on app version and user role, Food Truck Finder may collect or process Google Play data categories including Location, Personal info, Photos and videos, App activity, App info and performance, and Device or other IDs.
