# Privacy Policy for FTF: Food Truck Finder

**Effective date:** April 23, 2026  
**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

This is the source copy for the public privacy policy served at:

https://www.ftf-foodtruckfinder.com/privacy.html

Developer and privacy contact: Randy Trimble / Food Truck Finder, foodtruckfinderinfo@gmail.com.

Food Truck Finder collects and uses account information, role-specific profile information, location data, truck and event listing content, uploaded photos/media, push notification tokens, diagnostics, and usage information to provide food truck discovery, live truck locations, owner tools, organizer tools, event listings, favorites, alerts, maps, support, safety, and app reliability.

The app does not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app, including cloud hosting, authentication, storage, maps, app distribution, notifications, analytics, crash reporting, support, and AI/menu processing services when used.

Users can manage permissions in device settings, turn off Live Location when available, update profile/listing information in the app, and request deletion through the app or at:

https://www.ftf-foodtruckfinder.com/delete-account.html

The full HTML policy includes data collection, use, sharing, security, retention, deletion, children’s privacy, international processing, third-party links, and contact details.
