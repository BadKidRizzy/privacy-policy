Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing FTF Food Truck Finder. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account and profile information

  • Registration data such as name, email address, and profile details.
  • Authentication information needed to sign in with email or supported social logins.

Location data

  • Foreground location while the app is in use to show nearby trucks, directions, and distance.
  • Background location, if permission is granted, to support live truck location features.

Device and usage data

  • Device identifiers, operating system details, and crash logs.
  • Usage patterns that help improve product quality and feature decisions.

User content

  • Photos and uploaded images used for truck profiles or other supported content.
  • Ratings, reviews, and other content submitted through the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide nearby truck discovery, routing, and location-based features.
  • To support owner updates, menu visibility, favorites, and other product workflows.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and product experience.
  • To communicate product updates, promotions, and support responses when allowed.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We may disclose information when required by law or to protect safety and rights.
  • Data may transfer as part of a merger, acquisition, or business transaction.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update or delete profile information through the app where supported.
  • You can disable notifications in the app or device settings.
  • You may request access to or deletion of personal data where applicable by law.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is perfectly secure. Please contact us if you suspect unauthorized access.
