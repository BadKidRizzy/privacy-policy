Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for FTF: Food Truck Finder

Effective date: April 23, 2026
Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FTF: Food Truck Finder, also referred to as Food Truck Finder, collects, uses, shares, protects, retains, and deletes information when you use the Food Truck Finder mobile app and related web pages.

Developer and privacy contact: Randy Trimble / Food Truck Finder, foodtruckfinderinfo@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use the app, whether you are using Food Truck Finder as a foodie, food truck owner, or event organizer.

Account and profile information

  • Name, email address, account identifier, login provider, and authentication status.
  • Optional profile details, phone number, profile image, business address, organization name, and role type.
  • Account preferences, notification preferences, favorites, saved trucks, and support requests.

Location data

  • Food Truck Finder may collect approximate or precise device location while the app is in use to show nearby trucks, nearby events, distances, directions, and map results.
  • Food truck owners may enable Live Location. When Live Location is enabled by the owner, the app may collect and transmit the truck/device location in the background so customers can see where the truck is serving, even when the app is not open.
  • Users can disable location permissions at any time in device settings. Food truck owners can turn off Live Location in the app.

Truck, event, and public listing content

  • Truck names, descriptions, cuisines, menus, prices, schedules, deals, addresses, photos, social links, and operating status.
  • Event names, descriptions, dates, times, addresses, vendor counts, organizer details, event links, and event images.
  • Reviews, ratings, feedback, and other content users choose to submit.

Photos, media, and uploaded files

  • Truck photos, menu photos, profile images, and event images uploaded through the app or admin tools.
  • Metadata needed to store, display, moderate, and manage uploaded media.

Device, diagnostics, and usage information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, push notification token, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage events needed to provide app functionality, improve reliability, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide nearby food truck and event discovery, maps, directions, search, and distance features.
  • Allow owners to manage truck profiles, menus, photos, schedules, deals, and live location.
  • Allow organizers to create events, invite trucks, manage event details, and display event listings.
  • Allow foodies to view trucks, save favorites, receive alerts, review trucks, and find events.
  • Send account, favorite truck, event invite, and operational notifications when enabled.
  • Authenticate accounts, secure the app, prevent abuse, investigate bugs, and provide support.
  • Improve the app experience, reliability, safety, and performance.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We share information only as needed to operate, support, protect, and improve Food Truck Finder.

  • Public app listings: Truck profiles, event listings, menus, schedules, deals, public photos, public event details, and public reviews may be visible to app users.
  • Service providers: We use vendors that help operate the app, including cloud hosting, database, authentication, storage, maps, location search, analytics, crash reporting, push notifications, app distribution, and support tools.
  • Examples of providers: Firebase/Google Cloud, Google Sign-In, Google Maps and Places, Mapbox, Expo push notification services, Apple and Google app platform services, and AI/menu processing services when used to read or organize menu images.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information if required by law, to protect users, to investigate abuse, to enforce terms, or to protect our rights and services.

4. Secure Data Handling

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal and sensitive user data. These safeguards include access controls, authenticated accounts, encrypted transport where supported by service providers, managed cloud services, and restricted administrative workflows. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to protect user data and limit access to authorized purposes.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account information is generally kept while your account is active.
  • Truck, menu, schedule, event, media, review, favorite, and notification data is kept as needed to provide app features.
  • Location updates used for live truck discovery may be replaced or become stale as trucks update or stop sharing location.
  • Expired seeded events may be marked closed and deleted after a grace period. User-created event history may be retained unless deleted by the organizer or account owner.
  • Backups, logs, security records, fraud-prevention records, or legal records may be retained for a limited period when necessary.

6. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Food Truck Finder account from inside the app by opening the Profile tab and using the Delete Account option. You can also request deletion outside the app from our account deletion page: https://www.ftf-foodtruckfinder.com/delete-account.html.

When account deletion is completed, we delete or anonymize personal data associated with the account unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, or backup recovery purposes.

7. Your Choices

  • You can manage location, photo, and notification permissions in your device settings.
  • You can turn off Live Location in the app if you are a food truck owner.
  • You can update truck, menu, schedule, event, and profile information in the app where available.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data, subject to applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

Food Truck Finder is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

9. International Processing

Information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. Those locations may have data protection laws different from your jurisdiction.

10. Third-Party Links

Food Truck Finder may link to third-party websites, social profiles, map providers, payment pages, restaurant pages, or event pages. Those third parties are responsible for their own privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will change the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of Food Truck Finder after an update means the revised policy applies.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion help, contact: foodtruckfinderinfo@gmail.com.
